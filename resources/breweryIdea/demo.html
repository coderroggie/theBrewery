About Billy's Cantina

Billy's Cantina started back in 2007 when a few friends gathered for the first time in a dorm room in Saint Peter and shared a drink. If you were there, you know who you are. It was awesome.

Through the Years...

Billy's Cantina has continued over the last few years in an on-again off-again fashion. Due to space constraints, it occasionally gone on the road. It has gone as far and wide as Scotland, Denver, Duluth, San Diego, Williston, Memphis, Grand Forks, Fargo, San Jose, Bayfield, and, of course, Costa Rica.

Come and enjoy a nice time at the Cantina. Bring a friend. Giggity.
